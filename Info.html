Ya Wen

Photographer

Kia ora! I am Ya Wen. I started taking wedding photos in 2011. After experiencing many styles of wedding, I found what make photos special and impressive are always the real emotions caputred in them, the kind which comes and goes in flash and must be recorded in an instant. That’s why I love taking snap shoots and natural light shoots so much.

As a female photographer, I am sensitive enough to foresee the “big” moment occurs and be skilled enough to catch it for you and your family. I want to give you photos to look back at and remember your strong emotions in your special time.

My desire is whenever you look at the photo, the experience of happiness and love will be renewed in your heart and feel as if it happened only yesterday.

I believe that a good photographer is not the one who makes the photo stunning, but the one when stunning flare, will capture it forever.
